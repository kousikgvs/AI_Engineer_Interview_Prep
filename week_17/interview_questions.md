## Reasoning Models
1. Chain-of-thought as emergent behavior — what does that mean?
2. **Trained CoT vs prompted CoT — what's the difference?**
3. **Process reward models (PRMs) vs outcome reward models (ORMs) — when do you use each?**
4. What is o1-style reasoning (search over thought space)?
5. **What is test-time compute scaling and why does it matter?**
6. Majority voting vs best-of-N sampling.
7. How does Monte Carlo Tree Search apply to language models?

## Tool Use via RL
8. How do you train an agent to use tools via RL (not just prompting)?
9. Why is code execution a good verifiable RL environment?
10. What did Toolformer do?

## Agentic RL
11. What is credit assignment over long trajectories and why is it hard?
12. Exploration in agent RL — UCB / epsilon-greedy adapted for LLMs.
13. What is curriculum learning for agents?
14. Multi-agent RL — cooperation vs competition.

## Scientific Thinking
15. How do you design a rigorous ML experiment (hypothesis, IV, DV, controls)?
16. What is an ablation study? How do you test statistical significance?
17. What is the replication crisis in ML and what does it mean for your work?
18. How do you spot benchmark overfitting when reading a results table?

## Research Taste
19. Name papers that changed the field and *why* they mattered.
20. How do you tell a foundational paper from hype?

## Judgment / Design
21. **How would you design a training setup to teach an agent to use tools via RL?**
22. "PRMs or ORMs for this reasoning task?" — defend it.
